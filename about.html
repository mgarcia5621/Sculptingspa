What is Laser Removal?

Laser hair removal is a procedure that uses concentrated beams of light to remove unwanted hair. During the laser hair removal process at Dynamic Dermatology, your provider will use a laser to emit a light that will be absorbed by the hairâ€™s pigment. This light energy is then converted into heat, effectively damaging the hair follicles that produce hair in the affected area, which inhibits future growth. It should be noted that while this tried-and-true treatment method will work for long periods of time, it is not a permanent solution. Some people will experience a near permanent loss but for most people, some hair regrows eventually. The good news is that with this method you can have an effective solution that lasts for years and if there is regrowth, follow up treatment is quicker and more effective! Initially, the process takes a few treatments due to the different stages of the hair growth cycle. However, once this is accomplished you can expect no additional hair growth for a long time. Additionally, each subsequent treatment often extends the amount of time between sessions.
